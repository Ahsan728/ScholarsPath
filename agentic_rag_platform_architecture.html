Nationality-Based Filtering
Every opportunity is tagged with eligible nationalities, residence requirements, and exclusions. Applicants set their passport + residency in profile and only see relevant results.
Agentic Auto-Update
AI agents detect deadline changes, status updates (open/closed/extended), and new rounds automatically — no manual curation. Users get real-time alerts.
Conversational Search (RAG)
"Find me fully funded PhD positions in AI for Pakistani students in Europe closing after June 2025" — the LLM retrieves, ranks, and explains the best matches.
Smart Classification
Auto-tagged by: type (scholarship/funding/internship/PhD/postdoc), field, degree level, funding amount, host country, deadline urgency, and competitiveness score.
Deadline Alerts
Personalised reminders: 30/14/7/1 day before deadline via email, Telegram, or push notification. Includes AI-generated checklist of required documents.
Application Assistant
RAG-powered helper that answers "what documents do I need?", "how competitive is this?", summarises eligibility rules, and drafts SOP outlines.
Verified & Curated Layer
Community + admin can flag scam opportunities. AI scores source credibility. Verified badge for official institutional sources. Scam detection model trained on known fakes.
Multi-Channel Delivery
Web dashboard, mobile app, daily/weekly email digest, Telegram bot, Slack integration, and embeddable widget for university career portals.
